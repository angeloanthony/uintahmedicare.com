Uintah Basin Medicare provides local, licensed Medicare help throughout the Uintah Basin.

We help residents of Vernal, Roosevelt, Naples, Maeser, Jensen, Ballard, Fort Duchesne, and all of Uintah County compare Medicare Advantage, Medicare Supplement (Medigap), and Part D prescription drug plans.

Common Questions

Is there a cost for your help?

No. Our services are completely free to you. We're licensed and compensated by the insurance plans, so you receive expert local guidance at no charge.

Do you work with local doctors and hospitals?

Yes. We verify that your doctors, hospitals, and pharmacies are included in any plan we recommend. This includes Ashley Regional Medical Center, Uintah Basin Healthcare, and clinics throughout the Basin.

Can you help with my prescriptions?

Absolutely. We check your medication list against each plan's formulary to estimate your monthly costs and confirm coverage at pharmacies in Vernal, Roosevelt, and throughout Uintah County.
