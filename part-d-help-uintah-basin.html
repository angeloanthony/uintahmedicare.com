Common Part D Questions

When can I enroll in Part D?

You can enroll during your Initial Enrollment Period (when you first qualify for Medicare), Annual Enrollment Period (October 15–December 7), or certain Special Enrollment Periods. We'll review your specific situation.

What if my medications aren't covered?

We'll help you find plans that cover your drugs or work with your doctor to identify covered alternatives. We can also help you request exceptions if needed.

Does it cost anything for your help?

No. Our Part D plan comparison and enrollment services are completely free to you. We're compensated by the insurance plans.
