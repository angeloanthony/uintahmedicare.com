1. Information We Collect

We collect information to improve your experience on forebearfind.com. This includes:

5. Third-Party Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • With Google Analytics, as described above

Depending on your location (e.g., GDPR for EU residents, CCPA for California residents), you may have rights to:

By using our website, you consent to this Privacy Policy and our use of cookies and tracking technologies. You may withdraw consent by opting out of cookies or contacting us.
